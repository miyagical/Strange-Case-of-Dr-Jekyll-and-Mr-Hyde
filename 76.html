Activities After you read: Understanding 7 Answer the questions. 1 What does a servant girl witness one night? 2 What connects Mr Utterson with the two people involved in the event? 3 Why does Mr Utterson take the police to Soho? 4 What do they find there that is of interest? 5 Why is Mr Utterson upset by Mr Hyde's note? 6 What has caused Dr Lanyon's final illness? After you read: Speaking 8 Describe the appearances of Mr Hyde and Dr Jekyll. 9 Work in pairs. Take the parts of a policeman and the servant girl. Question the servant girl about the killing that she witnessed. Chapters 7-10 Before you read 10 Dr Lanyon left a letter marked "Not to be opened until the death or disappearance of Henry Jekyll". What do you think the letter contains? After you read: Understanding 11 Who is talking to whom?
