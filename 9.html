Introduction good. This is another subject that interested Victorians, and it is one that was discussed by many other writers. Dr Henry Jekyll is a well-respected and wealthy doctor in London. He does a great deal of good work and is liked by many, including his two oldest friends, Mr Utterson (a lawyer) and Dr Lanyon. The two friends are worried when they meet the unpleasant and mysterious Mr Hyde and discover that he seems to have some terrible influence on Dr Jekyll. A series of nasty events, ending in a murder, appear to be the responsibility of Mr Hyde. The friends decide to help Dr Jekyll, but as they do so they discover a secret of unimaginable horror.
